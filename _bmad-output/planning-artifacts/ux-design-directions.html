Dark-First Scientific
Two-ColumnData DensePrecision

SidebarSpatialBreadcrumbs

Color-Forward
GradientColor CardsVibrant
Dissector colors ARE the brand. Gradient borders, color-coded component cards, rainbow swatch strip. The most visually distinctive option.

6. Zenit

Balanced Professional
ToolbarSplit ViewMature
Clean toolbar, split view, status indicators. The "safe" choice — familiar desktop app conventions executed at high polish. Reliable and mature.
✨ Best for: Broadest appeal — familiar patterns for all users
